INTERNSHIP DURATION

  • 4 to 6 weeks for short-term, skill-based, or domain-specific internships

PROCESS OF INTERNSHIP

  • The internship program at MH Academy follows a structured and transparent process:
  • Orientation and awareness session regarding internship opportunities
  • Student registration and expression of interest
  • Selection of internship domain based on student background and interest
  • Assignment of internship tasks, learning objectives, and mentor guidance
  • Hands-on training, project work, or industrial exposure
  • Periodic monitoring, evaluation, and mentor support
  • Submission of internship report and completion documents
  • Issue of Internship Completion Certificate upon successful fulfillment of requirements
  • Students are required to complete a minimum number of working hours, as specified at the time of enrollment, to be eligible for certification.
